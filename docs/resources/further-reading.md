# Further Reading

Curated resources for deepening your robotics and AI knowledge.

## Books

### Robotics Fundamentals

#### Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control
- **Authors**: Kevin M. Lynch, Frank C. Park
- **Level**: Advanced undergraduate / Graduate
- **Topics**: Kinematics, dynamics, motion planning, control
- **Free PDF**: [Northwestern University](http://hades.mech.northwestern.edu/index.php/Modern_Robotics)
- **Why Read**: Mathematical foundation for robot manipulation and locomotion

#### Probabilistic Robotics
- **Authors**: Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, Dieter Fox
- **Level**: Graduate
- **Topics**: Localization, mapping, SLAM, Kalman filters, particle filters
- **Why Read**: Essential for understanding robot perception and state estimation

### ROS 2 and Software

#### Programming Robots with ROS 2
- **Authors**: Morgan Quigley, Brian Gerkey, William D. Smart (original ROS 1)
- **Status**: ROS 2 version in progress (check O'Reilly)
- **Topics**: ROS 2 architecture, nodes, packages, visualization
- **Why Read**: In-depth ROS 2 system design

#### A Gentle Introduction to ROS 2
- **Author**: Jason M. O'Kane
- **Free PDF**: Available online
- **Level**: Beginner
- **Why Read**: Accessible introduction with hands-on examples

### Machine Learning and RL

#### Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction (2nd Edition)
- **Authors**: Richard S. Sutton, Andrew G. Barto
- **Level**: Intermediate
- **Free PDF**: [MIT Press](http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html)
- **Topics**: RL fundamentals, Q-learning, policy gradients, actor-critic
- **Why Read**: The definitive RL textbook

#### Deep Learning
- **Authors**: Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville
- **Level**: Graduate
- **Free Online**: [deeplearningbook.org](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/)
- **Topics**: Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, optimization
- **Why Read**: Foundation for VLA models and vision systems

### Vision and Perception

#### Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications (2nd Edition)
- **Author**: Richard Szeliski
- **Level**: Undergraduate / Graduate
- **Free PDF**: [szeliski.org](http://szeliski.org/Book/)
- **Topics**: Image processing, feature detection, 3D reconstruction
- **Why Read**: Comprehensive computer vision reference

## Research Papers

### Vision-Language-Action Models

#### RT-2: Vision-Language-Action Models Transfer Web Knowledge to Robotic Control
- **Authors**: Brohan et al. (Google DeepMind)
- **Year**: 2023
- **Link**: [arXiv:2307.15818](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.15818)
- **Key Insight**: Pre-trained VLMs can ground language in robot actions

#### OpenVLA: An Open-Source Vision-Language-Action Model
- **Authors**: Kim et al.
- **Year**: 2024
- **Link**: [arXiv:2406.09246](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.09246)
- **Key Insight**: Open-source alternative to proprietary VLA models

### Reinforcement Learning for Robotics

#### Learning Dexterous In-Hand Manipulation
- **Authors**: Andrychowicz et al. (OpenAI)
- **Year**: 2020
- **Link**: [arXiv:1808.00177](https://arxiv.org/abs/1808.00177)
- **Key Insight**: Domain randomization enables sim-to-real transfer

#### Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms
- **Authors**: Schulman et al. (OpenAI)
- **Year**: 2017
- **Link**: [arXiv:1707.06347](https://arxiv.org/abs/1707.06347)
- **Key Insight**: PPO is the standard RL algorithm for robotics

### Simulation and Digital Twins

#### Isaac Gym: High Performance GPU-Based Physics Simulation
- **Authors**: Makoviychuk et al. (NVIDIA)
- **Year**: 2021
- **Link**: [arXiv:2108.10470](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.10470)
- **Key Insight**: Massive parallelization speeds up RL training 100x

#### Gazebo: A Multi-Robot Simulator for Outdoors and Indoors
- **Authors**: Koenig et al.
- **Year**: 2004
- **Link**: [IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1301938)
- **Key Insight**: Foundational paper for the Gazebo simulator

#### Unity Robotics Hub Documentation
- **Link**: [Unity Robotics Hub](https://github.com/Unity-Technologies/Unity-Robotics-Hub)
- **Key Insight**: Resources and packages for integrating Unity with ROS

## Online Courses

### ROS 2 and Robotics

#### ROS 2 Official Tutorials
- **Provider**: ROS.org
- **Link**: [docs.ros.org](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/Tutorials.html)
- **Level**: Beginner to Advanced
- **Cost**: Free
- **Why Take**: Official hands-on tutorials from ROS developers

#### Self-Driving Cars Specialization
- **Provider**: Coursera (University of Toronto)
- **Instructor**: Steven Waslander
- **Topics**: Perception, motion planning, control
- **Why Take**: Applied robotics for autonomous vehicles

### Machine Learning

#### Deep Reinforcement Learning
- **Provider**: UC Berkeley CS 285
- **Instructor**: Sergey Levine
- **Link**: [rail.eecs.berkeley.edu](http://rail.eecs.berkeley.edu/deeprlcourse/)
- **Level**: Graduate
- **Cost**: Free (lectures on YouTube)
- **Why Take**: State-of-the-art RL from leading researcher

#### Practical Deep Learning for Coders
- **Provider**: fast.ai
- **Instructor**: Jeremy Howard
- **Link**: [course.fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/)

- **Cost**: Free

## Datasets and Benchmarks

### Robot Learning Datasets

- **Link**: [robotics-transformer-x.github.io](https://robotics-transformer-x.github.io/)

- **Use Case**: Training VLA models

- **Link**: [robonet.wiki](https://www.robonet.wiki/)
- **Size**: 15M frames from 7 robot platforms
- **Use Case**: Multi-task robot learning

- **COCO (Common Objects in Context)**: Object detection, segmentation
- **ImageNet**: Image classification
- **Cityscapes**: Autonomous driving, semantic segmentation
